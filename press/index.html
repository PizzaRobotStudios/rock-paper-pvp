About the Game

Rock Paper PvP takes the world's simplest game and turns it into something you can't put down. Pick your champion. Choose your weapon. Battle your way up the leaderboards in fast, fierce best-of-3 duels set across 50 hand-crafted medieval arenas.

Every fight takes seconds. Every win streak feels legendary.

  • 30 unique champions with distinct personalities
  • 50+ battle arenas — forests, dungeons, taverns, castles, and more
  • Best-of-3 duels with escalating win streak rewards
  • 8-tier ranking system from Peasant to Champion
  • Full leaderboard with 50,000+ opponents to climb past
  • 5 exclusive color champions, 20 premium arenas, custom name colors with Premium ($0.99)
  • Original soundtrack with 20 tracks across combat and exploration zones
  • Haptic feedback, sound effects, and polished animations throughout
