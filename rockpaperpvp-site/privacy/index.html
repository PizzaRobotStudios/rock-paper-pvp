Privacy Policy — Rock Paper PvP

Effective date:

Rock Paper PvP is published by Pizza Robot Studios LLC (“we,” “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how Rock Paper PvP (the “App”) handles information.

Summary

  • We do not require accounts and we do not ask for your email or login.
  • The App may collect device/usage information for analytics and crash reporting.
  • The free version displays ads via Google AdMob.
  • Premium purchase (“Remove Ads”) is handled by Apple/Google in-app purchase systems.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

The App may allow you to choose a player name and icon. If you enter a name, treat it as public within the App (for example, on leaderboards). Please do not use personal information in your player name.

Automatically collected information

The App may collect certain information automatically, such as device identifiers, app interactions, and approximate location derived from IP (depending on ad/analytics settings). This data is used to operate the App, understand usage, and improve stability.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (ads in the free version). Ad partners may use identifiers to personalize ads depending on your device settings and region.
  • Google Firebase Analytics (usage analytics) to understand how the App is used.
  • Firebase Crash Reporting (Crashlytics) (stability/crash logs) to diagnose issues.
  • Apple / Google In-App Purchases for the Premium upgrade. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Third-party services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. Your use of those services is subject to their terms.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Measure performance and improve gameplay and UI
  • Diagnose crashes and bugs
  • Show ads in the free version (unless you purchase Premium)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers (such as ad and analytics providers) to operate the App and measure performance.

Children’s Privacy

Rock Paper PvP is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it where applicable.

Your Choices

  • You can limit ad tracking and personalization in your device settings.
  • You can choose not to enter personal information in your player name.
  • If you purchase Premium, ads are disabled in the App (subject to proper purchase restore).

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@rockpaperpvp.com.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at rockpaperpvp.com/privacy.
